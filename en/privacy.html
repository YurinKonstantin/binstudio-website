Privacy Policy for BinStudio

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the BinStudio application (hereinafter referred to as the "Application") handles your privacy and data processing.

1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

The BinStudio Application is designed as a completely local tool for working with binary files. We do not collect, store, track, or transmit any personal information, private data, cookies, or device identifiers.

2. Processing of User Files

All files that you open, analyze, or modify within BinStudio are processed strictly locally on your personal computer. The Application never uploads your files, their content, or metadata to external servers, cloud storage, or third-party resources.

3. Local Application Settings

The Application uses Windows system storage (LocalSettings) solely to save your local UI preferences, such as the selected theme (light/dark) and the successful save counter for the rating prompt. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services and Microsoft Store

The Application is distributed through the official Microsoft Store. Microsoft Corporation may collect anonymized telemetry (crash data, download counts) governed by Microsoft's own privacy policy. The Application does not integrate its own third-party analytical SDKs or advertising networks.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as the application's functionality evolves. Any changes become effective immediately upon their publication on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using BinStudio, you can contact the developer through the official project repository on GitHub.
